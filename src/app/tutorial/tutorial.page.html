Welcome

This app is for those who want to feel safe at all times, if you are in trouble you can keep press the charm to send an S.O.S. with your location

How it works

During the registartion you can insert up to 5 contats and in an emergency case, if you have a subscription, the operator will call all contacts setted during registration, operator can also call some public emergency numbers if you have enabled it in the registration phase.

Other features

If you usually forget how to take a medicine you can ask for a tag or a QRCode and simple scanning it you can see in the app all the procedure. In general you can personalize the code and see what you want

Ready to join?

You can sign up for free trial without subscription, but remember that in this case your alerts will be rejected!
